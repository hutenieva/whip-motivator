# Privacy Policy — Whip Motivator

**Last updated:** April 10, 2026

## Data Collection

Whip Motivator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the extension stores

The extension uses Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) to save two preferences on your device:

- Whether the whip is toggled on or off
- Your selected hit mode (Neutral, Loving, Angry, or Mixed)

This data never leaves your browser.

## Network requests

The extension makes zero network requests. It does not communicate with any server, API, or third-party service.

## Permissions

- **activeTab**: Used to inject the whip canvas overlay onto Google Meet pages
- **storage**: Used to save your toggle and mode preferences locally

## Third-party services

None.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at https://github.com/hutenieva/whip-motivator/issues
